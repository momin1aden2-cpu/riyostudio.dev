Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

The TL;DR on Zero-Server Privacy: The tools on Riyo Studio (File Forge, AI Scanner, QR Hub, Invoice Maker) are designed to run entirely in your web browser. We do not upload, process, or store your JSON data, PDF documents, cover letters, invoices, or QR code payloads on any remote servers. Your data never leaves your device.

1. Introduction

Riyo Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the riyostudio.dev website and its associated suite of developer and business tools. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). We are a registered business operating in Western Australia.

2. The Data We Do NOT Collect

Because our core tools are built with a "client-side first" architecture, we fundamentally do not have access to the data you process using them. Specifically:

  • File Forge: JSON data, CSV data, JWT tokens, and images processed in the forge are handled strictly by your local browser memory.
  • AI Scanner: The text, resumes, and documentation you paste for analysis are scanned locally.
  • QR Hub: The URLs, vCard details, WiFi passwords, and Crypto addresses used to generate QR codes are processed locally.
  • Invoice Maker: Client details, financial data, and business logos reside in your browser and are generated into a PDF on your device.

3. The Data We May Collect

We only collect data when you actively provide it to us outside of the client-side tools, or through standard web infrastructure:

  • Contact Forms: If you use the "Get in Touch" form, we collect your name, email address, and message in order to respond to your inquiry.
  • Analytics & Logging: Like most websites, our hosting provider automatically logs standard connection data such as IP addresses, browser types, and access times for security and performance monitoring.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies for targeted advertising. We may use your browser's localStorage or sessionStorage to save your UI preferences (like the dark mode toggle or command palette history). This data remains on your device.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We will only disclose information if required to do so by Australian law (e.g., via a court order).

6. Your Rights

Under the APPs, you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to ask that we correct it. Since we do not retain user data from our tools, this generally only applies to direct email correspondence or contact form submissions.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our zero-server architecture, please contact us via the form on our homepage.
